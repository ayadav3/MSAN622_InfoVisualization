Discussion

The bubble chart displays information about four variables. The size of the circle reflects the population of a state. The color of the cirlce represents the region. The x and y axis represent the percentage of graduates and the per capita income respectively. I removed the borders for the circles to decrease the clutter. It is interesting to note that Alaska has the highest per capita income and a very high percentage of graduates. The scatterplot matrix represents the interaction among the number of murders, percentage of graduates and life expectancy.
