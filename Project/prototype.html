Continent-wise Exports by India (USD)

  • Sort by value

Dataset Summary

The dataset contains information about country-wise India exports by principal commodity in 2011-12 and 2012-13. I modified the original dataset by adding continent and commodity category columns. I choose this dataset to see what all products are exported by India and who are the major importers of indian products.

Sketch Summary

I will be implementing 4 techniques on the choosen dataset.

  • Interactive Bar Plot

    • I will use the value or quantity of exports on the y-axis and the continents on the x-axis. As of now, the dataset does not have a column 'Continent'. I will add this column to the dataset soon. Also, I plan to use filters for selecting the commodity and financial year. I will add a check box to sort the bars. I hope to learn concepts of filtering and sorting.

  • Interactive World Map

    • I will use the world map to show the country wise exports of commodities. I plan to use filters to select a commodity and year of exports. The user can select any commodity to see the countries which imported it. On mouse hover, a tooltip will be displayed to show the name of the country and the actual value of export. I hope to learn the use of geo coordinates in D3.

  • Small Multiples (Bar Plot)

    • I plan to use small multiples of bar plots to visualize the change in exports of different commodity categories over the two financial years.

  • BiPartite

    • I plan to use a Bipartite visualization to see the different categories of commodities exported to countries in the two financial year. I will add categories for the commodities to the dataset.
