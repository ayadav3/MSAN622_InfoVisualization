Discussion

The Multi-Series Line Plot displays the number of deaths or serious injuries for the drivers, front seat passengers and rear seat panssengers in UK from January 1969 to December 1984. On mousehover event one can see the exact number of deaths or injuries for that point in time.

I chose these three variables as they are naturally related to each other. The drivers are more likely to get killed or injured than the other front seat passenger. Similaryly, the front seat passengers are more likely to get killed or injured than the passengers sitting on the rear seats.
