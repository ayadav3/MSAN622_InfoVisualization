User Guide

Hover over the circles to get details about the location, magnitude and depth of the earthquake. The details are displayed at the bottom of the map in italics. Also, the size of the circle is proportional to the magnitude of the earthquake.
Double click or scroll down on the map to zoom in. Scroll up on the map to zoom out. Click and drag to see the panning functionality.
